How it works

Your online school in 3 simple steps

Share ideas

Show us your educational materials and describe what you want your interactive courses to look like.

Let us do the job

Give a name to your future online school and we do the rest: we turn your materials into high-quality interactive courses and place them in a user-friendly learning management system hosted on a dedicated virtual server.

Get the result

You get a ready-to-go online school filled up with your own interactive courses. The only thing you need to do is to set the price for your educational content and let your students create accounts to get access to the courses they paid for.
